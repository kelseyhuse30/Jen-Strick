MEET JEN /

Jen Strickland has been shooting photography in the Philly area since winter of 2014, when her thoughtful husband gifted her a Nikon D3300 for Christmas as a complete surprise. Jen is described by her friends as a "bundle of joy", "forever smiling and making others laugh", and "always supportive". She has an ability to make anyone feel comfortable and at home. Her talent to sense a person's true personality and style enables her to capture a unique portrait for all types of people and purposes. Contact her today for a fun and artistic photography experience.
